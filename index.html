Classic League & Super League

  • These league competitions will last for 38 weeks. (i.e. They are two separate private leagues)
  • Managers of the week and month will receive ₦1,500 & ₦5,000 respectively
  • At the end of the season, the top three managers will receive ₦100,000, ₦50,000, & ₦25,000 respectively and the winner of the Super League will win ₦20,000.
  • 4th and 5th place will receive a jersey of their choice as compensation.
  • Also at the end of the season, the top 4 players from the classic league will be promoted to the “Super League” and the last 4 from the current “Super League” will be eliminated.

All Black FPL Cup

  • This cup competition is automatically created by FPL themselves within the Classic league.
  • It’s a knockout competition amongst all the players.
  • The winner of the cup at the end of the season will receive ₦20,000.

All Black Elimination Chamber

  • This is a competition created by the All Black League on its own.
  • It’s another private league in which the managers with the lowest scores each week will be eliminated until it is remaining 3 players at the top.
  • They will receive ₦5,000, ₦3,000 & ₦2,000 respectively.
  • There will be three different series (i.e. GW8-GW18, GW19-GW28 & GW29-GW38).
  • Note: you must be registered in the Classic league to participate in this competition.
  • More information will be given in the WhatsApp group upon commencement of the season.
